# Prizes
***
We will award the monetary prizes to the top performers in either phases of the competition.

The top three performers will be awarded the following prizes:

🥇 First Place: $2,000

🥈 Second Place: $1,500

🥉 Third Place: $500

Note that a single user or team will only be awarded one prize. Eligibility is determined by the Terms and Conditions that all participants must agree with to enter the challenge.

In addition to monetary prizes, we will evaluate models for which a short write-up and example code will have to be submitted. The models will be evaluated in terms of score, practicality, frugality in terms of resources and novelty. Special jury prizes will be determined when the public phase of the competition is over.
